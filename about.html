Brandt Hackney’s experience as a cinematographer spans over a decade, including work in various genres of film and music video. His chief goal as Director of Photography is to create images that affect the viewer on an emotional and subliminal level in service of the work’s vision. As he shapes and discovers light, focusing on composition and expertly capturing movement, the narrative is brought to life with vivid depth. Travelling throughout the United States and internationally, Brandt is available for shooting drama, commercials, documentaries, and music videos. His passion for quality filmmaking is evident through his solid constitution and drive for excellence, no matter the project's size or scope.
