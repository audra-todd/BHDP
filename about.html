Brandt Hackney’s experience as a cinematographer spans over a decade, including 10 feature films and dozens of music videos. His chief goal as Director of Photography is creating images that affect the viewer on an emotional and subliminal level in service of the work’s vision. As he shapes and discovers light, focusing on composition and expertly capturing movement, the narrative is brought to life with vivid depth.

Based in Orlando and travelling throughout the United States and internationally, Brandt is available for shooting drama, commercials, documentaries, and music videos. His passion for quality filmmaking is evident through his solid constitution and drive for excellence, no matter the project's size or scope.
